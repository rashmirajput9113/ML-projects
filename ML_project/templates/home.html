Old Bike Price Prediction

Introduction

Welcome to the Old Bike Price Prediction project. This platform is designed to help users estimate the market value of used bikes based on various features such as model, year, condition, mileage, and more.

Features

  • Predict the price of used bikes accurately.
  • Analyze trends in bike pricing.
  • User-friendly interface for easy data input.
  • Comprehensive dataset covering various models and brands.

How It Works

Enter the details of the bike you want to sell or buy, and our model will predict the most likely price based on historical data and machine learning algorithms.
